STEPAuthorize

Sign and Verify STEP files with ease
download .ZIPdownload .TGZ

Usage:

STEPAuth.bat SIGN <file to sign> <private key> <certificate>

-Outputs signed_<file to sign> which contains a signature, public key and certificate at the end.

STEPAuth.bat VERIFY <signed file>

Outputs Verification OK or Verification Failure.

Included:

  • Sample Private Key privatekey_signing-ca.key
  • Sample Certificate Certificate_signing-ca.cert
  • Sample signed file signed_AS1-AC-214.stp
  • Sample Certificate Authority chain of trust /ca
